Second Opinion
Urology

At Nightingale Hospital, our Urology Department is dedicated to providing advanced care for disorders of the urinary tract and male reproductive system. With cutting-edge technology and a team of experienced urologists, we ensure accurate diagnosis, effective treatments, and compassionate care.

Our Expertise

We specialize in treating a wide range of urological conditions, including:

  • Kidney Stones: Comprehensive care for diagnosis, removal, and prevention of kidney stones.
  • Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs): Advanced treatment for recurring and complicated UTIs.
  • Prostate Disorders: Management of benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH), prostatitis, and prostate cancer.
  • Bladder Conditions: Expertise in treating bladder infections, incontinence, and bladder cancer.
  • Male Infertility: Diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting male reproductive health.

Services We Offer

  • Kidney Stone Management
    • Advanced techniques like shockwave lithotripsy (SWL), ureteroscopy, and laser treatments for stone removal.
    • Comprehensive dietary guidance to prevent recurrence.
  • Prostate Care
    • Minimally invasive procedures for enlarged prostate (BPH) and prostate cancer.
    • Expert diagnosis and management tailored to individual needs.
  • Bladder and Urinary Health
    • Treatment for incontinence, overactive bladder, and urinary retention.
    • Bladder reconstruction and advanced therapies for complex conditions.
  • Male Reproductive Health
    • Expertise in varicocele repair, erectile dysfunction, and other fertility-related conditions.
    • Hormonal therapies and microsurgical techniques.
  • Urological Cancer Care
    • Comprehensive management of kidney, bladder, prostate, and testicular cancers.
    • Multidisciplinary approach to diagnosis, treatment, and recovery.
  • Minimally Invasive Urology
    • Laparoscopic and robotic-assisted surgeries for precise and effective treatments.
    • Faster recovery times and reduced post-surgery discomfort.
