Laparoscopic Surgery

Nightingale Hospital is proud to offer advanced laparoscopic surgery, a minimally invasive surgical technique designed to reduce recovery time, minimize pain, and enhance precision. Our team of expert surgeons, supported by cutting-edge technology, ensures safe and effective outcomes for a variety of conditions.

What is Laparoscopic Surgery?

Also known as keyhole surgery, laparoscopic surgery involves the use of a thin, flexible tube (laparoscope) with a camera and specialized instruments inserted through small incisions. This allows surgeons to perform complex procedures with greater accuracy and minimal disruption to surrounding tissues.

Conditions We Treat

  • Gallbladder Diseases: Gallstones and cholecystitis.
  • Hernias: Inguinal, umbilical, and incisional hernia repairs.
  • Appendicitis: Removal of an inflamed appendix.
  • Gastrointestinal Disorders: Management of ulcers, adhesions, and other GI conditions.
  • Gynecological Conditions: Ovarian cysts, fibroids, and endometriosis.
  • Urological Issues: Kidney stone removal and adrenal gland surgeries.

Advantages of Laparoscopic Surgery

  • Smaller incisions and minimal scarring.
  • Reduced pain and discomfort post-surgery.
  • Faster recovery and shorter hospital stays.
  • Lower risk of infection and complications.
