Dr. K. Vijaya Shekar Reddy

A Legacy of Excellence

Dr. K. Vijaya Shekar Reddy, A Pioneering Surgeon

With a distinguished career spanning over three decades, Dr. K. Vijaya Shekar Reddy has established himself as a leading figure in the field of surgery. A proud alumnus of the prestigious Osmania Medical College, where he earned his MBBS degree in 1986, Dr. Reddy has consistently demonstrated a passion for delivering exceptional patient care and advancing the frontiers of surgical science.

A Master Surgeon

Dr. Reddy's academic prowess and dedication to his craft were further honed with the completion of his MS in General Surgery from the renowned Kasturba Medical College (KMC) in Manipal, in 1990. This solid foundation in surgical principles and techniques has enabled him to excel in his practice, earning the respect and admiration of his peers and patients alike.

A Visionary Entrepreneur

In 1992, Dr. Reddy took a bold step by founding Nightingale Multi-Specialty Hospital, a state-of-the-art medical facility that has become a benchmark for quality healthcare in the region. This pioneering effort not only showcased his entrepreneurial spirit but also underscored his commitment to providing comprehensive and compassionate care to his community.

A Laparoscopic Surgery Pioneer

Dr. Reddy has been at the forefront of minimally invasive surgery, embracing the revolutionary technology of laparoscopy since 1995. As one of the earliest adopters of this technique, he has performed thousands of successful laparoscopic procedures, demonstrating his exceptional skill and dexterity. His expertise in this area has benefited countless patients, who have experienced reduced recovery times, minimal scarring, and improved outcomes.

A Legacy of Trust and Excellence

Throughout his illustrious career, Dr. K. Vijaya Shekar Reddy has consistently demonstrated a deep commitment to his patients, his profession, and his community. His dedication to delivering personalized, high-quality care has earned him a reputation as a trusted and compassionate surgeon.

  • Performed thousands of open and laparoscopic surgeries with exceptional success rates
  • Established a renowned multi-specialty hospital
