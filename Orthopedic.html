Second Opinion
Orthopedic

At Nightingale Hospital, our Orthopedic Department is dedicated to providing comprehensive care for all musculoskeletal conditions. With a team of experienced orthopedic surgeons and advanced technology, we ensure personalized treatment plans to help our patients lead pain-free, active lives.

Our Expertise

We specialize in diagnosing and treating a wide range of orthopedic conditions, including: 

  • Bone Fractures: Expert care for simple to complex fractures, including surgical and non-surgical options.
  • Joint Disorders:Comprehensive treatments for arthritis, bursitis, and other joint issues.
  • Sports Injuries: Advanced care for ligament tears, sprains, and tendon injuries to ensure a faster recovery.
  • Spine Disorders:Specialized treatment for back pain, herniated discs, scoliosis, and more.
  • Pediatric Orthopedics: Caring for musculoskeletal conditions in children with gentle and effective methods.

Services We Offer

  • Joint Replacement Surgery
    • Knee, hip, and shoulder replacements using minimally invasive techniques.
    • Customized implants for better fit and comfort.
  • Arthroscopy
    • Minimally invasive procedures for diagnosing and treating joint issues.
    • Ideal for sports injuries and chronic joint pain.
  • Trauma Care
    • Emergency services for fractures, dislocations, and other orthopedic emergencies.
    • 24/7 support from skilled surgeons and trauma experts.
  • Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation
    • Post-surgery care plans for faster recovery.
    • Personalized therapy sessions to restore mobility and strength.
  • Spinal Care
    • Innovative treatments for chronic back pain, spinal deformities, and injuries.
    • Expertise in minimally invasive spine surgeries.
