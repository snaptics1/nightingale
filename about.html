About Nightingale Multispecialty Hospital

Your Trusted Partner in Health and Wellness

Established in 1992, Nightingale Multispecialty Hospital began as a small setup and has grown into a 100-bed facility, offering comprehensive healthcare services. The hospital specializes in General Surgery (including Laparoscopic procedures), Gynecology & Obstetrics, General Medicine, Orthopedics,and other allied specialties. Recognized by the State Government, Nightingale Hospital is renowned for delivering quality healthcare to the lower and middle-class communities at affordable prices.


With decades of service, the hospital has built a loyal clientele and a strong reputation for excellence. Its team of highly skilled consultants is well-regarded in the region for their expertise. Equipped with advanced diagnostic tools and life-saving equipment, Nightingale Hospital continues to set a benchmark in providing reliable, compassionate care.
