A Legacy of Care

Dr. Lalitha Reddy, A Renowned Obstetrician and Gynaecologist

With a career spanning over three decades, Dr. Lalitha Reddy has established herself as a leading figure in the field of obstetrics and gynaecology. A proud alumnus of the prestigious Gandhi Medical College, where she earned her MBBS degree in 1990, Dr. Reddy has consistently demonstrated a passion for delivering exceptional patient care and advancing the frontiers of women's health.

A Distinguished Career

Dr. Reddy's academic prowess and dedication to her craft were further honed with the completion of her DGO in 1993. This solid foundation in obstetrics and gynaecology has enabled her to excel in her practice, earning the respect and admiration of her peers and patients alike. As a co-founder of Nightingale Multi-Specialty Hospital, Dr. Reddy has played a pivotal role in shaping the hospital's vision and mission, ensuring that it remains a beacon of hope and healing for countless women and families.

A Master Surgeon

Over the last three decades, Dr. Reddy has performed thousands of obstetric and gynaec procedures, including normal deliveries, caesarian sections, and caesarian hysterectomies. Her expertise in difficult obstetric procedures has been a lifeline for many patients, and her vast experience in gynaec procedures such as open hysterectomies, vaginal hysterectomies, and cystocoele rectocoele repairs has earned her a reputation as a skilled and compassionate surgeon. Dr. Reddy is also well-versed in gynaec laparoscopic procedures, including total laparoscopic hysterectomy, ovarian cystectomy, and laparoscopic surgery for ectopic pregnancy.

A Full Circle of Care

What sets Dr. Reddy apart is her unique experience of delivering patients who were born in her hands. This full circle of care is a testament to her dedication and commitment to her patients, and a reminder that her work is not just about delivering babies, but about nurturing families and building relationships that last a lifetime.

A Leader and a Visionary

Dr. Reddy is not only an exceptional clinician but also an active participant in the day-to-day management of Nightingale Hospital. Her leadership and vision have been instrumental in shaping the hospital's growth and development, and her commitment to excellence has inspired a generation of healthcare professionals.

A Legacy of Trust and Excellence

Throughout her illustrious career, Dr. Lalitha Reddy has consistently demonstrated a deep commitment to her patients, her profession, and her community. Her dedication to delivering personalized, high-quality care has earned her a reputation as a trusted and compassionate obstetrician and gynaecologist. As a testament to her outstanding contributions to the field of women's health, Dr. Reddy continues to be a respected and sought-after medical professional, known for her unwavering commitment to excellence, innovation, and patient care.
